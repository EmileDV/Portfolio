My projects

In this part, I present a few projects I worked on. These are of course not the only ones but they are the ones that I am most proud of. The first 2 projects were school tasks and the last 2 were personal projects. I am currently working on the last one.

Yembis
Qlick - Azure Database - Angular

Yembis was a project where all students from all 4 directions of the IT factory would work together to work out different projects. With my team, we worked out a proof of concept for a budget traffic camera with advanced analysis of data. Our task as App development students was to create a database and represent the data in a way that is easy to understand and useful.

Ypto hackathon
Power Apps

My team and I went to the Ypto hackathon of 2022 where we won 1st place in the expert track. We created a prototype for the digital inventorization of all parts for all wagons. Since we won, we got to go to Paris with a personal tour of the Microsoft EU Headquarters. After our tour, we had a weekend reserved at a hotel so we could visit the city ourselves.
